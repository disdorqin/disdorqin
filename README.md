## 👋 About

> **AI Engineer building the infrastructure behind reproducible research** — from forecasting models to autonomous literature pipelines.

I design and ship **research-builder systems**: tooling that turns raw data and papers into reproducible, monitorable experiments. My work sits at the intersection of **time-series ML**, **energy systems**, and **LLM-powered research automation**.

- 🔭 Currently building **[DARIS](https://github.com/disdorqin/DARIS)** — an autonomous research workflow engine
- 🛠️ Maintaining **[TSPLab](https://github.com/disdorqin/tsplab)** (forecasting audit & benchmark) and **[research-claw](https://github.com/disdorqin/research-claw)** (AI paper pipeline)
- 🌱 Exploring LLM agents for end-to-end research automation
- 💬 Ask me about forecasting pipelines, experiment reproducibility, and research tooling
